Formulary: Six Hops From Molecule to Adverse Event

How AION traced a MACE signal through a six-hop causal chain — from a stereochemistry decision in 2021 to a black-box warning in 2024 — and sealed every link as auditable, signed evidence.

Primitives9 MCP tools Nodes12 stages Hops6 causal Elapsed3 years, 4 months

The Problem: Drug Safety Is a Six-Hop Problem

A synthesis decision leads to an impurity profile. An impurity profile changes bioavailability. A bioavailability shift masks CYP3A4 saturation. Masked saturation becomes a drug-drug interaction in combination therapy. That interaction triggers fourteen MACE events spread across three post-market studies. Those events become a black-box warning. No single investigator traces the full chain. No single database holds all six layers. No existing pharmacovigilance system connects the decision room in 2021 to the warning label in 2024.

AION can. Every stage of drug development — from molecule design through post-market surveillance — is ingested as a signed node. Every causal relationship is encoded as a typed, directed edge. When a MACE signal appears, aion_path reconstructs the full chain in a single traversal. Every hop is auditable. Every claim is signed.

The substrate identified the contradiction automatically. No manual comparison of documents was required.

Why This Matters

The FDA does not have a system that traces synthesis decisions to adverse events six hops and three years later. Pharmacovigilance databases hold adverse event reports. Chemistry systems hold synthesis records. Clinical trial registries hold enrollment data. None of these systems talk to each other at the causal level.

AION does. Every node is signed. Every edge is typed. Every causal claim is auditable. When a black-box warning appears, you can trace it all the way back to a stereochemistry choice made in 2021 — and produce a tamper-evident evidence record that a regulator, a lawyer, or a pharmacovigilance committee can independently verify.

The six-hop chain was not assembled by a human reviewer spending weeks cross-referencing documents. It was returned by aion_path in a single query. The contradiction between the 2022 label and the 2024 MACE signal was not caught in a manual audit. It was surfaced by aion_contradictions automatically. The causal substrate makes the invisible chain visible — and seals it as evidence.
